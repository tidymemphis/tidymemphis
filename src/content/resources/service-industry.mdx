# File for Funemployment
Go to the Tennessee Department of Labor's website [here](https://www.jobs4tn.gov/), click <b>Unemployment Benefits</b>, then <b>File a Claim</b> to see if you are eligible for unemployment benefits. You are able to file immediately after you lose your job.

# [Memphis Food Industry Emergency Worker Assistance](https://www.gofundme.com/f/memphisfoodworkers)

A gofundme by Edible Memphis. Complete the confidential survery, which will be used to prioritize those most at risk and in need. They plan to start with $250 to $500 disbursements, which will be continually reevaluated, pending availability and circumstances.
